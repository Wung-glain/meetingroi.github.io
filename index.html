What is MeetingROI?

MeetingROI is an AI-powered analytics platform that provides insights into meeting productivity, participation quality, and real-time cost analysis. By factoring in attendee salaries, agenda clarity, and meeting outcomes, MeetingROI helps you transform meetings from time-wasters into growth engines.

Key Features

  • Real-time meeting cost estimation
  • Productivity and outcome prediction
  • AI-based improvement suggestions
  • Employee feedback and analysis integration
  • Custom reports for HR and Management
